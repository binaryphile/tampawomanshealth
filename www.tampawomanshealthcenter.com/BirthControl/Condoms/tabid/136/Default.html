The male condom is a sheath that fits over the erect penis. It is open at one end and usually has a small reservoir at the closed tip. This type of condom comes in a variety of sizes, some with special features such as ribs, ultra thin, lubrication, etc.
 
Typically, condoms are made of latex or polyurethane.
 
Latex condoms are the most popular. These can be used with water based lubricants, but should not be used with Vaseline or oil based lubricants such as cold cream as they break down the latex.
 
For men, and their partner who may be allergic to latex, the polyurethane condom is a better choice. They are a bit thinner than the latex condoms, may require a bit more lubrication and are somewhat less flexible.
